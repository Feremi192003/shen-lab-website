About the lab

Science at the intersection of multiple disciplines

The Shen Group is based in Pharmaceutical Sciences at the University of Maryland. Our work brings together chemistry, biology, physics, medicine, and computer science.

We investigate molecular mechanisms and develop computational methods that help researchers understand biological systems, design therapeutics, and create responsive materials.
