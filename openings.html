Who should apply

Strong candidates may come from chemistry, biophysics, pharmaceutical sciences, physics, computer science, applied mathematics, or related fields.

  • Graduate students seeking interdisciplinary PhD research
  • Postdoctoral researchers developing or applying computational methods
  • Undergraduates interested in research training
  • Collaborators with complementary experimental or computational expertise
