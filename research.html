How we work

Methods connected to biological and chemical questions

Our projects combine molecular dynamics, constant-pH methods, free-energy calculations, electronic-structure descriptors, machine learning, and experimental collaboration.

Each research direction links method development with a question where molecular-scale insight can change how a system is understood or designed.
