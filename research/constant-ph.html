Method development

Constant-pH molecular dynamics

A molecular simulation should respond when the solution pH changes.

Overview

Continuous constant-pH molecular dynamics (CpHMD) couples protonation-state sampling to conformational dynamics. Instead of assigning every ionizable group a fixed state before a simulation begins, CpHMD allows those states to evolve with molecular structure and environment.

How can we build an accurate, reliable, and computationally efficient pH-stat for biomolecular simulation?

Research program

What we investigate

01

Implicit-solvent CpHMD

GB-Neck2 methods enable efficient pKa prediction and proton-coupled conformational sampling on CPUs and GPUs.

02

All-atom explicit solvent

Hybrid-solvent, generalized-reaction-field, and particle-mesh-Ewald formulations increase physical realism.

03

Enhanced sampling

pH replica exchange and asynchronous exchange improve convergence across coupled protonation and conformational landscapes.

04

Community software

Methods are implemented and disseminated through widely used molecular-dynamics packages, including Amber and CHARMM.
